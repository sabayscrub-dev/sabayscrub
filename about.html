About Us

Sabay Scrub

Sabay Scrub is designed to make everyday cleaning easier, safer, and more enjoyable. Unlike ordinary kitchen sponges, Sabay Scrub features a fun smiling-face design with smart cleaning functions that help clean dishes more effectively while protecting your cookware from scratches.

Our sponge is:

  • Soft in warm water for gentle cleaning
  • Firm in cold water for stronger scrubbing
  • Scratch-free and safe for non-stick surfaces
  • Designed with a smile shape to clean spoons and utensils easily
  • Comfortable to hold and easy to use
  • More hygienic and durable than ordinary sponges

We created Sabay Scrub to bring better-quality kitchen cleaning products to Cambodian homes at an affordable price.


Because even small tools can improve everyday life.

Founder Story

Sabay Scrub began from a real part of my everyday life.


My family has four members, and I am the oldest brother. Since we do not have household helpers at home, my younger brother and I share the responsibility of washing dishes every day. My brother usually cleans in the afternoon, while I wash dishes after dinner in the evening.


Because we use kitchen sponges every day, I realized that many sponges available in Cambodia are low quality. They wear out quickly, smell bad after a short time, and are not very hygienic.


One day, while watching Shark Tank, I discovered a unique sponge product that was very different from normal sponges. It was designed to clean better, last longer, and protect kitchenware without scratching surfaces.


Unfortunately, quality sponges like these were difficult to find locally, and we often had to import them from other countries, which made them expensive due to shipping costs and import fees. Having experience sourcing products from China, I was familiar with the process, so I started researching better materials and looking specifically for high-quality sponges that could meet our needs.


That idea became Sabay Scrub.


Our goal is to provide Cambodian families with hygienic, durable, and affordable cleaning sponges.


Because better cleaning should be available for everyone.


Sok Visal
Founder & CEO of Sabay Scrub

Sabay Scrub is designed to support better kitchen hygiene and human health by effectively cleaning dishes while preventing bacteria buildup, ensuring utensils are safe, clean, and hygienic for everyday use.
